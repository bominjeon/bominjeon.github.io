Nemesis

Brainstorming and sketches:



My nemisis is Chipotle. I developed and designed the identity for Ricelydone, Korean restaurant which would compete with Chipotle with healthier food options.

Typeface: Letter Gothic stc and Santa Fe LET Plain 1.
The menu describes how to build a bowl of deliciousness starting from rice to toppings. There are two major dishes which are bibimbop and dupbop.
